NSTP Activities

Explore the key activities and experiences from our NSTP program during the first semester.

Red Cross CPR Training and Disaster Preparedness Seminar

It was our first time attending a Red Cross seminar, and the entire experience was truly memorable. Unfortunately we don't have a picture with the speaker. The seminar was engaging, informative, and full of valuable lessons. We had the opportunity to practice CPR, which we really enjoyed. The seminar was more than just a regular learning session. It taught us skills that could be helpful in unpredictable accidents or emergencies.

Cybercrime Prevention and Anti-Hazing Seminar

Attending the Cybercrime Prevention and Anti-Hazing Seminar was very informative. The seminar taught us about the risks of online crime and the consequences of hazing. We gained a lot of knowledge on how to keep ourselves safe and make responsible choices.

Mental Health Awareness

The Mental Health Awareness Seminar was an eye-opener for us, as we learned the importance of taking care of our mental health. We realized that we should be open about our feelings and pay attention to the well-being of our loved ones.
